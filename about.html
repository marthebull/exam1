ABOUT US

Here at extinct, we write about the most extensive challenges associated with the extinction of animals and the most endangered animal species we have on this planet. This varies from climate challenges to man-made extinction situations. This is the only planet we have and we must make it a safe place for both humans and animals. Nature depends on us taking responsibility. We use this blog as a platform to spread knowledge about what we can do to take care of the earth and how you can help fight extinction. No one can save everyone, but everyone can save someone.

WANT TO CONTRIBUTE?

Get in touch if you want guidance on what you can contribute in your area or where you can go to help. There are endangered species all over the world, both animals and plants. There are also many conservation organizations around the world that greatly appreciate your contribution either in the form of donations or labor.
